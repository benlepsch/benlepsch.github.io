Hello and welcome to my website! I built this mainly to catalog my projects, and so that I could reference my own website on job applications. I hope you enjoy!



Who am I?

I'm Ben. I recently graduated as a Rodman Scholar from the University of Virginia Engineering School with a degree in Computer Science, and I'm looking for a job in software / web development. In my free time, I enjoy rock climbing, playing guitar and banjo, reading, and working on old cars.

My Projects

  • Bean Jump: I wrote this browser-based clone of Papi Trampoline in high school. It briefly became such a hit that I had multiple teachers approach me about kids in their classes playing Bean Jump instead of paying attention.
  • benbot and benbot-rs: My friends and I ran out of space to pin messages in our Discord server, so I wrote a bot in Python that can save messages to a separate channel. I've also been learning Rust, so I recently rewrote the bot in Rust to help me learn the syntax.
  • AGR: This is a website (still in development) cataloging some popular rock climbing routes in Acadia National Park.
  • Kyriakos Run: This is a simple Chrome (and Firefox) extension infinite runner game, featuring Kyriakos Grizzly.
